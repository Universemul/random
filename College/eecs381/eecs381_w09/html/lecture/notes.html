Lecture Notes

Note: This page links to lecture outlines. They will be provided only when feasible and useful. Lecture outlines are my raw outlines. I can quickly put these on the web site, but they are not written out, like a book or handout. The topics are not necessarily in order, or all repesented, or all covered in lecture. The outlines will have typos, though they should be basically correct. Outlines that apply to multiple lecture days may replace earlier versions, so recheck this list.

In general, this material will not be comprehensible without the lecture; it is provided to supplement, not replace, the lecture presentation and help compensate for our large crowded classrooms and my crummy handwriting. For example, you might want to correct your notes of code examples. The example code is also available in the course afs directory:

/afs/umich.edu/class/eecs381/examples

If the title of the lecture notes includes an asterisk(*), then the posted version corresponds to the final version used in lecture. If the asterisk is not present, the posted version may be obsolete - I am constantly revising my notes, hopefully for the better. Watch for an update.
